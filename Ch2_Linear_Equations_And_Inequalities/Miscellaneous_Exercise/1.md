> i) $\frac{3}{7} x - \frac{1}{4} = -\frac{4}{7}x - \frac{5}{4}$


$\frac{3}{7} x - \frac{1}{4} = -\frac{4}{7}x - \frac{5}{4}$

$\Rightarrow \frac{3}{7} x + \frac{4}{7}x  = \frac{1}{4} - \frac{5}{4} \quad \quad ...\text{adding} \: \frac{4}{7}x + \frac{1}{4} \: \text{on both sides}$

$\Rightarrow x = -1$
