> A natural number is less than the sum of its opposite and $8$. Find 
> all such numbers. 

--------------------------------

Let $n$ be the natural number we are looking for. 

$n < (-n) + 8$

$\Rightarrow 2n < 8$

$\Rightarrow n < 4$

Since $n$ is a natural number the solution set is $S.S = \{1, 2, 3\}$.
