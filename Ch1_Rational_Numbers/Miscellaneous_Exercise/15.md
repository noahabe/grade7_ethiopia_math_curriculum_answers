> Some number is added to itself. The result is multiplied by $5$ and the 
> product is $15$. What was the number? 

--------------------------------

Let's call the number $x$. Translating the question into an 
equation we get: 

$
5 (x + x) = 15
$

$
\Rightarrow 5 (2x) = 15
$

$
\Rightarrow 10x = 15 
$

$
\Rightarrow x = 1.5 
$

Thus the number is $1.5$.
