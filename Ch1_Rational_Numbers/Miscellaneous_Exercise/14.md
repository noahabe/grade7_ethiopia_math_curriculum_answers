> Adding $3$ to some number, then multiplying the result 
> by $7$ gives $28$. What was the original number? 

--------------------------------

Let's call the number $x$. Translating the question into an 
equation we get: 

$
7(3 + x) = 28 
$

$
\Rightarrow 3 + x = 4
$

$
\Rightarrow x = 1
$

Thus the original number is $1$.
