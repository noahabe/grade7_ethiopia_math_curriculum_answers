> e. $3x^2 + 2 (5x + 3x^2)$

$3x^2 + 2 (5x + 3x^2)$

$= 3x^2 + 10x + 6x^2  \quad \quad  \text{Distributive property}$

$= 3x^2 + 6x^2 + 10x  \quad \quad  \text{Commutative property of addition}$

$= 9x^2 + 10x  \quad \quad  \text{Computation}$
